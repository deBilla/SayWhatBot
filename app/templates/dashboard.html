How to create a Telegram bot

  1. Open Telegram and search for @BotFather.
  2. Send /newbot and follow the prompts (pick a name and a username ending in bot).
  3. BotFather replies with a token like 123456789:ABCdefGhIjK... — copy it.
  4. Paste the token above and click Connect bot.
  5. Open your new bot and send it a voice note. 🎉

Only one transcription runs at a time across the whole server, so during busy periods your request may be queued for a few seconds.
